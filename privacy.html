Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

CYBERXP8 Draft Uploader is a private/internal tool used by the account owner to upload CYBERXP8 videos to TikTok as drafts or inbox uploads for manual review and posting.

Information We Collect

When you connect TikTok to CYBERXP8 Draft Uploader, the tool may receive limited authorization information from TikTok, such as an access token, refresh token, TikTok open ID, granted scopes, upload status, and basic responses needed to complete draft uploads.

The tool does not ask for, collect, or store your TikTok password.

How We Use Information

We use TikTok authorization information only to:

  • authenticate the account owner with TikTok;
  • upload selected CYBERXP8 video files to TikTok as drafts/inbox uploads;
  • check upload status and troubleshoot upload failures;
  • maintain a local record of which CYBERXP8 drafts were prepared.

Permissions Requested

The intended TikTok permission for this tool is video.upload. This permission is used to upload content for manual review/posting by the TikTok account owner. The tool is not intended to request direct publishing permission unless the account owner separately chooses to enable it later.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share TikTok authorization data with third parties. Data is used only for the CYBERXP8 Draft Uploader workflow and TikTok API communication required to perform draft uploads.

Data Storage and Security

Tokens and configuration values are stored locally in the account owner's environment and should not be posted in public chats, source repositories, or public documents. Reasonable technical safeguards should be used to protect local credential files.

Data Retention

Authorization data is retained only as long as needed to operate the draft uploader. The account owner may remove local tokens at any time or revoke TikTok app access through TikTok account settings.

User Control

The TikTok account owner controls whether uploaded drafts are edited, published, or deleted inside TikTok. CYBERXP8 Draft Uploader does not automatically publish videos when configured only with video.upload.

Children's Privacy

This tool is not directed to children and is intended for use only by the CYBERXP8 account owner or authorized operator.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the draft uploader workflow changes. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the CYBERXP8 account owner through the official CYBERXP8 contact channel or website where this policy is hosted.
